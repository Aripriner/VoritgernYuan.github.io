My Summer Plan

Posted on 2022-06-24 In Plan

Happy Summmer Vacation!

This is my first summer vacation in Peking University. After one year’s study, I gradually realize that my mathematical ability is so poor. There is a long distance between me and those brillant student.

So in this summer, I decide to build up this blog to encourage myself to wirte down my feelings and ideas.I can also push up myself to read these “big” books.

Let’s go back to my summer plan. In the coming semester, I will take these courses:

  • Algebraic Geometry
  • Algebraic Number Thoery
  • Basic Topology
  • Abstract Algebra
  • Mathematical Analysis
  • (possible) homological theory

So I want to read these books to help myself successfully finish them:

  • Bott-Tu, Differential Forms in Algebraic Topology
  • J.P.May, A concise course in Algebraic Topology
  • Milnor, Characteristic Classes
  • Milnor, Singular Points of Complex Hypersurfaces
  • Donaldson, Riemann Surfaces
  • Liu Qing, Algebraic Geometry and Arithmetic Curves
  • Hartshorne, Algebraic Geometry
  • Rudin, Real and Complex Analysis
  • Rudin, Functional Analysis
  • Milne, Galois Theory
  • Milne, Algebraic Number Theory
  • Serre, Linear representations of Finite Groups

It seems too many…

I will update my notes and summaries later(maybe).
